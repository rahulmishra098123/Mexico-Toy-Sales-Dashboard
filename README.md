 ## Project Objective

- The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance of a Mexico-based toy retail business using Power BI.

 This analysis focuses on identifying:

- Overall business performance

- Product-level profitability

- Store-level performance

- Sales trends over time

- Location-based sales contribution

- The goal is to convert raw sales data into meaningful insights that can support business decisions.


 ##  Key Business Questions (KPIs)

- What is the total Sales, Cost, and Profit?

- How many Units were sold?

- How are Sales and Profit changing over time?

- Which products generate the highest profit?

- Which stores contribute the most profit?

- Which store location type performs best?


 ## Data Process

- Data Cleaning & Preparation

  Checked and corrected data types (Date, Numeric, Text).

  Verified null values and ensured data consistency.

  Formatted currency fields properly.

  Ensured date column was in correct format for time analysis.

-  Data Modeling

 Created relationships between tables.

 Built a dedicated Date Table using DAX for time intelligence.

   ## Key Insights

- The business generated 14.44M in total sales with a 4.01M profit, showing healthy profitability.

- Sales show growth from 2022 to 2023, especially in Q1 and Q2 2023.

- Colorbuds is the highest profit-generating product, followed by Action Figures and Lego Bricks.

- A small number of products contribute a large share of total profit.

- Downtown store locations generate stronger sales compared to other location types.

- Some stores significantly outperform others, indicating possible regional demand differences.


  ## Business Recommendations

- Increase focus on high-profit products like Colorbuds through promotions and inventory planning.

- Analyze low-performing products to optimize pricing or reduce stock.

- Study top-performing store locations to replicate their strategy in weaker branches.

- Use quarterly trends to plan marketing campaigns around strong sales periods.
